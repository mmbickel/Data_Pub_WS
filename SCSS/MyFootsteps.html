My Footsteps

by Lydia Davis, 2014.

I see myself from the back, walking. There are circles of both light and shadow around each of my footsetps. I know that with each step I can now go farther and faster than ever before, so of course I want to spring forward and run. But I am told that I must pause at each step, letting my foot rest on the ground for a moment, if I want it to develop its full power and reach, before taking the next. dream
