Up Now: Kacey Slone

jan 28th - feb 25th

Kacey Slone

Slone’s work investigates identity, memory, and how place affects one's belonging. By using familiar objects, Slone hopes to connect with others through collective memory. In this recording she discusses her process and methodology for selecting objects and imagery. She also introduces her biography and connection to her rural upbringing, her move to Texas for graduate school, and the impact of both of those experiences, as important contributions to her process.
